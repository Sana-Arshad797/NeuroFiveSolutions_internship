Built from the reusable template in prompt-template.md. Below are 5 prompts generated from that same template (only variables changed), tested in an AI tool, with saved outputs.

Prompt 1 — Delayed Order

Filled Prompt:

Role: You are a friendly, professional customer support agent for Northwind Retail.

Context: A customer has written in about their order being delayed. Relevant details: 
order #48213, expected 3 days ago, no tracking update since dispatch.

Task: Write a reply that acknowledges the delay and reassures the customer their order 
is being looked into.

Format: 3-4 sentences, warm but professional tone, written as a direct reply to the customer.

Constraints: Don't promise a specific delivery date. Avoid technical jargon.

Output:

Hi there, thank you for reaching out, and I'm sorry to hear order #48213 hasn't arrived yet. I understand how frustrating it is to be waiting without any tracking updates. I've flagged your order with our logistics team so we can get you the latest status as soon as possible. We appreciate your patience and will follow up the moment we have news.

Prompt 2 — Refund Query

Filled Prompt:

Role: You are a friendly, professional customer support agent for Northwind Retail.

Context: A customer has written in asking why their refund for a returned item hasn't 
shown up yet. Relevant details: item returned 10 days ago, refund confirmation email 
received, but no funds in their account.

Task: Write a reply that answers their question clearly and explains next steps.

Format: 3-4 sentences, warm but professional tone, written as a direct reply to the customer.

Constraints: Don't promise a specific refund date. Avoid technical jargon.

Output:

Hi, thanks for your patience while your refund processes. Since your return was confirmed 10 days ago, the refund should be on its way, though bank processing times can sometimes add a few extra days on their end. If it still hasn't appeared soon, I'd recommend checking with your bank first, and we're happy to look into it further from our side too. Let us know if you need anything else in the meantime.
